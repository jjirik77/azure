A Brief History of AI

1950 - The Turing Test

Alan Turing proposes a test for machine intelligence, a cornerstone of AI philosophy.

1956 - The Birth of AI

The term "Artificial Intelligence" is coined at the Dartmouth Conference.

1997 - Deep Blue vs. Kasparov

IBM's Deep Blue chess computer defeats world champion Garry Kasparov.

2012 - AlexNet Revolution

A deep neural network called AlexNet drastically improves image recognition, sparking the deep learning boom.

2018 - Transformer Models

Google introduces BERT, a transformer-based model that revolutionizes natural language processing.

2022-Present - Generative AI Explosion

Models like GPT-3, DALL-E 2, and Stable Diffusion make high-quality generative AI widely accessible.
